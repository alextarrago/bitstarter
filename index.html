FAQ

What is Klosy and why is good for me?
Klosy is a little hardware device and a iOS/Android app for knowing the status of your doors. Worried about your main entrance when you are in vacations? Did you forgot to close your fridge's door? Klosy can solve this by notifying you directly on your phone.
How Klosy work?
You just have to set up a Klosy in your desired door by connecting it in a Wifi network. Once you have connected them just check the status of your doors in your phone and start adding some rules for them. For example, with Klosy app you can trigger a notification, or you can start a buzzer/alarm once a specific door is opened.
What differs from a regular alarm system?
Klosy doesn't intend to supersede the regular alarm systems. Is just an additional device for home security. A regular alarm system can't notify you when the fridge's door is opened (right?). Klosy adds some security improvements to your current security system at home. Check out the description for learning some uses we though for Klosy.
